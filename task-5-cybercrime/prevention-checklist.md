Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

•	Always check properly before accepting any UPI request, sometimes “receive money” is actually payment. 
•	Do not click on unknown links from WhatsApp, SMS or email, they can be fake or harmful. 
•	Avoid scanning random QR codes, specially from unknown people or messages. 
•	Use strong passwords and try to change them time to time for better security. 
•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for important apps like banking and email. 
•	Do not use public Wi-Fi for payments or logging into sensitive accounts because it is not safe. 
•	Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store, not from random links. 
•	For any suspicion, immediately report to National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.
•	If you get any suspicious call asking for money or details, don’t trust it and verify first.
